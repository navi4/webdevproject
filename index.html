About Us

With more than 15 years of experience, we are one of the oldest and well-known tour companies in Uganda. We have our fleet of 4WD Toyota Land Cruisers, custom build for safaris. We also are partner with Nature Lodges, accommodations located in the most beautiful places. Not 5 star hotels, but with great views, fantastic food, decent and comfortable. We are an Ugandan company, run by an Ugandan/Dutch couple. We are proud to have revived the tourism to Uganda and that over all the years we had thousands of happy visitors. They came for the mountain gorillas, but were surprised with all the other great parks and nature in Uganda.
